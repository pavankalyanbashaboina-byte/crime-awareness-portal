Committees Handling These Crimes

Learn about the internal authorities responsible for addressing various misconduct and crimes.

Internal Complaints Committee (ICC)

The ICC is responsible for handling complaints related to sexual harassment and ensuring a safe environment for all members of the college community.

  • Handles sexual harassment cases
  • Ensures confidentiality
  • Provides support to victims

Anti-Ragging Squad

The Anti-Ragging Squad monitors and prevents ragging incidents on campus, taking swift action against any form of ragging.

  • Monitors ragging activities
  • Conducts awareness programs
  • Imposes penalties on offenders

Disciplinary Committee

This committee oversees general disciplinary matters, including academic misconduct, vandalism, and other violations of college rules.

  • Reviews disciplinary cases
  • Imposes academic penalties
  • Promotes ethical conduct
