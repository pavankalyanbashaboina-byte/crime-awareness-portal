⚡ Action System

Click "Take Action" on any report in the Reports section to resolve a case and notify the student. The student will receive a notification with your action message.

How it works:

  1. Go to View Reports and find the case
  2. Click ⚡ Take Action button
  3. Type the action taken (e.g. "Student warned, 3-day suspension issued")
  4. Click Send to Student
  5. The student will see the notification when they login
